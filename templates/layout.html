How it Works

First, we convert your audio file into a set of spoken sentences. Then, we run textual sentiment anlaysis on these sentences to determine textual emotion behind each sentence. Finally, we do sentiment analysis using just your voice in the audio file. We score you in two ways: percentage of sentences for which your voice emotion matches the text emotion, and also a proprietary score that measures how close your emotions were to the text's emotions. This proprietary score is out of 10,000 with 10,000 being perfectly matching. Good luck practicing!
